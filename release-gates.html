Release Gates / 2026-05-14

GCA Product Release Gates

This page defines what must be true before GCA AI Quant Access is described as a public self-service product. The current state is public product spec only, not a live trading terminal, not live market data, and not financial advice.

Public Account UI is not live. Credits are not self-service claimable. GCA Member status is not self-service claimable. The 10,000 GCA member benefit is not self-service claimable. Any product claim must stay inside the gates below until implementation, support review, and disclosures are live.

Safe To Say

  • GCA has published product release gates for GCA AI Quant Access.
  • The current public page is a product spec and release-gate disclosure.
  • Public self-service credits, GCA Member status, and the 10,000 GCA member benefit require controlled account UI, read-only wallet verification, 30-day holding-period review, ledger activation, and support review.
  • Any future trading-related feature must pass risk-control review and simulation or testnet first.

Do Not Claim

  • Do not claim credit claiming is live before controlled account UI and credit ledger activation.
  • Do not claim GCA Member status is live before controlled account UI and member ledger activation.
  • Do not claim the 10,000 GCA member benefit is live before 30-day holding verification and member ledger activation.
  • Do not claim the product gives custody, withdrawal permission, exchange API secret collection, platform revenue distribution, trading permission, or risk-control bypass.
  • Do not claim BaseScan token profile approval, external audit completion, deep liquidity, or tracked listing approval before publication.
