Security & Transparency

The contract source is verified on BaseScan and the repository includes tests and an internal engineering review. Third-party audit quote requests were submitted to QuillAudits, Hacken, and OpenZeppelin on 2026-05-10, then deferred by owner decision. No third-party audit has been completed.

Fixed Supply

The full 1,000,000,000 GCA supply was created at deployment. The contract has no post-deployment mint function.

No Admin Controls

The token contract has no owner/admin role, proxy or upgrade path, pause switch, blacklist, transfer tax, hidden fee, custody, or withdrawal path.
