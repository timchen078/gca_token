Supply

Total supply is fixed at 1,000,000,000 GCA. The contract has no post-deployment mint function.

Owner Reserve

600,000,000 GCA is held in the disclosed owner reserve wallet. It is a normal owner-controlled wallet, not a lock, vesting contract, or multisig.

Audit

Internal review is complete. No third-party audit has been completed, and audit quote requests were deferred by owner decision.
