GCA currently stands for Go China Access, a concept-stage community direction focused on the Go China macro narrative, bilingual Web3 education, public market research, and planned access to non-custodial quant risk tools. The intended Web3 Radar bridge is an access and membership layer for reports, risk-warning credits, backtests, ENTRY_READY signal review, position-size calculators, subscription discounts, and risk-control training. The contract creates 1,000,000,000 GCA at deployment and includes no post-deployment minting, burning, taxes, blacklist, or admin controls.
