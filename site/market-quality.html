Operating Principles

  • Use the GCA/USDT pool as the single public market route.
  • Improve market quality through transparent liquidity, useful product delivery, and legitimate public participation.
  • Disclose starter-depth liquidity, owner reserve custody, external review status, and third-party audit status plainly.
  • Do not use artificial activity, self-trading, wash trading, misleading volume, or paid listing promises as a trust strategy.

Quality Position

The current priority is not a tracked-listing push. It is keeping the public identity consistent, moving users to the official GCA/USDT route, disclosing starter-depth liquidity plainly, and connecting utility pages to account workflows as the product matures.
