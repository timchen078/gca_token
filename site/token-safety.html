Boundaries

Risk, Market Quality, and Listing Readiness define what should not be claimed yet.

Public Claim Boundaries

  • Safe to say: GCA is source-verified, fixed-supply, and has no mint, no token tax, no blacklist, no proxy, no pause, no owner/admin role, and no withdrawal path in the token contract.
  • Do not say: wallet-warning removed, warning-free, or claim warning removal before visible confirmation from Blockaid / MetaMask. Also do not claim externally audited, audit-approved, locked reserve, deep liquidity, price support, or listing approved before those facts are visible from the relevant third party.
  • Do not ask users for private keys, seed phrases, exchange API secrets, withdrawal permission, one-time codes, custody, or fund transfers.
