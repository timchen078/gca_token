Reviewer Notes

  • This page is intended to answer holder-concentration and reserve-disclosure questions.
  • The reserve is disclosed as a normal owner-controlled wallet, not hidden circulation.
  • No LP lock is currently claimed. If a future LP lock is executed, it should be documented with exact platform, lock address, transaction hash, unlock date, and custody details.
  • Do not describe the reserve as locked, vested, multisig-controlled, burned, or externally audited unless that later becomes verifiable on-chain or through an independent report.
