Risk Factor Response

Price Volatility

Acknowledged. GCA has starter-depth liquidity, so swaps can have high price impact and volatile execution prices. Public materials disclose this and do not claim price stability or price support.

LP Lock

No LP lock is currently claimed. The Liquidity Statement records the unlocked LP boundary and the evidence required before any future LP lock claim can be made.

Supply Concentration

The 600,000,000 GCA owner-held reserve is publicly disclosed at 0x5e8F84748612B913aAcC937492AC25dc5630E246 with two on-chain transfer proofs. The Holder Distribution page records that concentration risk remains because the reserve is owner-controlled and not currently locked, vested, or multisig-controlled.

Third-party Audit

No third-party audit has been completed. The internal technical report is for reviewer triage and should not be described as a Certik, Hacken, Trail of Bits, or other independent audit report. The Audit Readiness page lists the future audit scope and handoff checklist.
