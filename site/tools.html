Workspace Boundaries

Browser onlyCalculations use values entered on the current device.
No executionThe tools do not connect exchanges or place orders.
No credit deductionPublic previews do not change account credit records.

Workflow order is an operating checklist, not a buy or sell signal and not proof that a trade or strategy is safe. The workspace does not fetch live market data, connect wallets, monitor positions, or override risk controls.
