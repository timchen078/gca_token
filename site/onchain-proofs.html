Public Claim Boundaries

  • Safe to say: GCA was deployed on Base Mainnet and source verification is complete on BaseScan.
  • Safe to say: GCA has fixed total supply and no post-deployment mint function.
  • Safe to say: owner reserve transfer transaction hashes are public.
  • Safe to say: the official public market route is the Base Mainnet Uniswap v4 GCA/USDT pool.
  • Safe to say: No third-party audit has been completed.
  • Do not say the reserve is locked, vested, or multisig-controlled before on-chain custody changes.
  • Do not say exact circulating supply has been accepted by data platforms before publication.
  • Do not claim mature liquidity, price support, return promises, warning-free status, or third-party audit completion.
