GCA Risk Disclosures

This page is the public risk reference for GCA. It does not tell anyone to buy or sell. It explains the known limitations around liquidity, wallet warnings, supply reporting, owner reserve custody, audits, utility readiness, and public communications.

Market and Liquidity

The official GCA/USDT pool on Base Mainnet has starter-depth liquidity only. Small trades can move price sharply, execution can vary, slippage can be high, and data platforms may show delayed or incomplete price data.

Wallet and DEX Warnings

Wallets and DEX interfaces can show high-risk warnings for new, low-liquidity, or lightly indexed tokens. A Blockaid false-positive report was submitted on 2026-05-10, but report submission does not mean warnings have been removed.

Audit Status

Internal engineering review is complete, but no independent third-party audit report exists. GCA should not be described as externally audited or audit-approved.

Reserve Custody

The 600,000,000 GCA owner reserve is held in a disclosed owner-controlled wallet. It is not locked, vested, or multisig-controlled unless custody changes on-chain later.

Utility Readiness

The Web3 Radar access bridge is still not public self-service from this website. Holder credits and GCA Member status are planned service-access records, not cash, tokens, income, reimbursement, or trading permission.

Supply Reporting

The contract total supply is fixed at 1,000,000,000 GCA. The 40/60 allocation is a public reporting target. Data platforms can apply their own circulating-supply methods and may not immediately accept the reserve treatment.

Listing and Metadata

GeckoTerminal token information was approved on 2026-05-11, while the BaseScan token profile is still awaiting review. CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap tracked listing requests are not submitted because current liquidity and public activity are still weak.

Do Not Use These Claims

  • Do not claim price support, yield, redemption rights, guaranteed liquidity, deep liquidity, or investment outcomes.
  • Do not claim third-party audit completion, external audit approval, locked reserve, or multisig reserve control.
  • Do not claim wallet warnings are removed until the wallet or security vendor confirms it in practice.
  • Do not use artificial volume, self-trading, wash trading, or fake activity as a listing or trust strategy.
  • Do not ask users for private keys, seed phrases, exchange API secrets, withdrawal permission, or custody of funds.

Safer Public Framing

Use verifiable facts: Base Mainnet chain ID 8453, the official token contract, source verification, fixed supply, no mint function, no token tax, official GCA/USDT pool, disclosed owner reserve wallet, GeckoTerminal approval, pending BaseScan profile, no third-party audit, and starter-depth liquidity.
