Project Direction

GCA currently stands for Go China Access. The intended direction is to build a community and product layer for people who follow China-facing Web3 narratives and need better trading discipline, risk review, and research tooling. The practical utility is still being shaped, so public materials describe it as a planned product path rather than a finished platform.

What It Is Not

GCA is not an investment product, does not promise price performance, does not distribute platform revenue, and has not completed a third-party audit. Public claims should stay focused on verifiable facts and roadmap progress.

Verified Utility: Web3 Radar Access Layer

The strongest use case is not simply holding a token. GCA can become the user entry point for a non-custodial quant trading toolkit: users keep funds in their own exchange accounts, while the platform provides research, backtesting, risk controls, and educational workflows.

Risk Reports

Use GCA membership or credits to unlock liquidation replay reports, mistake reviews, and market-risk briefings.

Tool Credits

Use GCA as an access layer for risk-warning credits, backtest runs, ENTRY_READY signal review, and position-size calculators.

Education

Use GCA for risk-control training, trading discipline lessons, and bilingual Go China market narrative research.

Holder Bonus

First campaign backend: each registered user may verify one wallet holding at least 10,000 GCA and receive a one-time 100 Web3 Radar utility credits ledger record.

GCA Member

Member tier backend: a registered user who verifies one wallet holding at least 1,000,000 GCA can qualify for GCA Member status in the Web3 Radar member ledger.

This utility roadmap does not include platform revenue distribution, return guarantees, custody, or automatic live trading permissions. Utility credits and GCA Member status are service-access benefits for reports, backtests, risk warnings, reviews, calculators, education, member research notes, and priority support only; they are not cash, tokens, income, reimbursement, or trading permission, and they are not a reason to bypass risk controls. Public claiming should launch only after Web3 Radar exposes the verified backend through controlled HTTPS account UI and public disclosures.

Macro Narrative

Risk Tools

Access

A long-term path toward curated resources for users trying to understand Chinese market narratives and avoid unplanned, emotional trading.

Access Bridge Pipeline

The current build path is a conservative user funnel: collect pre-registration interest, verify queued wallet balances in the Web3 Radar backend, then record service credits and GCA Member status in append-only ledgers. The public static page remains manual until a controlled HTTPS endpoint and account UI are released.

1. Pre-Register

Users submit email, Telegram, Base wallet, declared GCA balance, and program intent. The public page does not request private keys, seed phrases, exchange secrets, withdrawal permissions, or custody.

2. Review Queue

Web3 Radar has local intake, wallet verification, 100 credits, and GCA Member ledgers. Submission alone creates a pending record; verified balances create service-access ledger records only.

3. Product Access

After public account UI is released, verified users can be mapped to reports, backtests, risk warnings, ENTRY_READY reviews, calculators, education, member notes, and priority support.
