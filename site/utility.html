GCA Utility Thesis

The Go China narrative remains the public macro story. The product direction is to connect that narrative to a real user problem: crypto traders lose money through liquidation, over-sizing, revenge trading, weak backtesting, and missing risk controls.

GCA can become the access layer for Web3 Radar-style non-custodial quant tools. The token should unlock useful workflow access, not promise returns or distribute platform revenue.

One Sentence

GCA is the Go China macro-narrative token planned to give users access to risk reports, backtests, warnings, signal review, position sizing, and trading discipline education inside a non-custodial quant platform.

Boundary

The platform should never require withdrawal permission, hold user funds, promise outcomes, or let token ownership bypass risk checks. Any live trading feature must stay behind testnet, approvals, and risk gates.

Suitable Token Utility

Reports Liquidation replay reports

Use credits or membership to unlock structured post-trade reviews and liquidation replay reports.

Risk Warning credits

Use risk-warning credits for risk alerts, crowding checks, volatility warnings, and position review.

Research Backtest runs

Use access credits for realistic backtests with fees, slippage, drawdown, and sample-quality checks.

Signals ENTRY_READY signal review

Unlock reviewed signal candidates without allowing direct live execution or return claims.

Sizing Position calculator

Use calculators for risk budget, stop distance, leverage bounds, and exposure limits.

Education risk-control training

Unlock lessons about liquidation prevention, trade journaling, and disciplined execution.

Membership GCA Member status

Backend-supported service tier for registered users who verify one wallet holding at least 1,000,000 GCA through the Web3 Radar access bridge.

Verified Holder Programs

Each registered user may verify one wallet holding at least 10,000 GCA and receive a one-time 100 Web3 Radar utility credits ledger record.

A registered user who verifies one wallet holding at least 1,000,000 GCA can qualify for GCA Member status, with possible access to higher utility credit limits, member research notes, priority report queue, member training sessions, and priority support after the public account UI is released.

These programs are account-level service access, not token rebates. Credits and member status should only apply to reports, backtests, risk warnings, ENTRY_READY signal review, position calculators, education access, member research, or support workflows; they must not be cash, tokens, income, reimbursement, trading permission, or a reason to bypass risk controls.

Rollout Plan

Phase 1: Public Thesis

Publish the GCA x Web3 Radar utility thesis and keep all claims clearly marked as planned utility.

Phase 2: Off-chain Access

Web3 Radar backend records wallet balance verification, one-time holder credits, and GCA Member status in append-only ledgers.

Phase 3: Public Account UI

Expose the verified backend only after controlled HTTPS deployment, privacy rules, account UI, and support workflows are ready.

This page describes a product thesis and verified utility backend path. GCA does not represent custody, redemption rights, platform revenue distribution, return guarantees, or automatic trading permission. Trading tools can reduce process mistakes, but they cannot remove market risk.
