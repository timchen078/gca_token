Pre-Registration Only

The public member page can generate a local pre-registration packet. It does not create a live Web3 Radar account, does not make credits self-service claimable, and does not automatically activate GCA Member status.

Account-Level Service Access

Planned 100 credits and GCA Member status are account-level service-access records for reports, backtests, risk warnings, ENTRY_READY signal review, position calculators, education access, member notes, training, and priority support.

No Custody Or Withdrawal Permission

GCA and Web3 Radar do not require private keys, seed phrases, exchange API secrets, withdrawal permission, or custody of funds. Wallet verification is intended to be a read-only ERC-20 balance check.

No Outcome Promise

GCA public materials are not financial advice and do not promise price, liquidity, listings, wallet-warning removal, audit approval, trading results, live trading permission, or risk-control bypass.
