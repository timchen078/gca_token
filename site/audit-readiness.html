Auditor Handoff Template

Please review the GCA fixed-supply ERC-20 contract and deployed bytecode on Base Mainnet. The goal is to confirm that the verified source matches deployed bytecode, the total supply is fixed, and no hidden privileged control, minting, withdrawal, blacklist, tax, or upgrade behavior exists.

Expected Deliverables

  • Auditor name and report date.
  • Final report URL or signed PDF.
  • Reviewed source bundle or commit hash.
  • Unresolved findings, if any.
  • Clear statement that the review is independent.

Do Not Overstate

  • Do not say third-party audit completion before an independent final report is public.
  • Do not say auditor approval before the auditor publishes or signs a final report.
  • Do not say this package removes the need for an independent audit if a reviewer requires one.
