Safety Boundary

This public static page does not store data on a server. The packet stays in your browser until you copy, download, or email it. When a reviewed HTTPS endpoint is connected, it may store only the pre-registration review fields, then Web3 Radar may verify the queued wallet with an ERC-20 balanceOf read. Pre-registration is not financial advice, not a token rebate, not cash, not income, not reimbursement, not trading permission, and not a way to bypass Web3 Radar risk controls.

100 Credit Rules

The planned holder bonus is a one-time account-level service credit record for verified 10,000 GCA holders. Credits are intended for reports, backtests, risk warnings, ENTRY_READY signal review, position calculators, and education access. They are not transferable and cannot be redeemed for cash, tokens, income, reimbursement, or trading permission.

Member Rules

The planned GCA Member tier is service access only for verified 1,000,000 GCA holders. Member status can support higher utility limits, member research notes, priority report queue, member training sessions, and priority support. It does not activate live trading, leverage, custody, withdrawals, voting control, lifetime access, or risk-control bypass.

Support Workflow

After controlled HTTPS intake is live, support can review packets through the statuses received, wallet_pending, eligible, needs_more_information, rejected, and ledger_recorded. The target first response is 5-10 business days after controlled intake is live, and it is not a guarantee. Manual support cannot override wallet-balance verification, risk controls, or the no-custody boundary.
