About GCA

GCA, short for Go China Access, is a Base Mainnet fixed-supply token project with a public narrative, live account and member intake, and ten browser-only risk and research tools for China-facing Web3 workflows. Connected market-data and trading modules remain staged.

The public project direction is GCA AI Quant Access: turning the Go China macro narrative into readable research, risk education, liquidation replay, backtesting, ENTRY_READY review, position sizing, and member-access workflows.
