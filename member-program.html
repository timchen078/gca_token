GCA Member Program

The GCA member program connects registered accounts, read-only wallet balance verification, utility credits, and GCA Member review.

The current rule: buying and holding 1,000,000 GCA for one month is the eligibility path for GCA Member review, and approved members can enter a one-time 10,000 GCA member benefit review.

Flow

How The Program Works

Register an email account

Use the email registration page or member access page. Do not submit private keys, seed phrases, wallet passwords, exchange API secrets, or withdrawal permissions.

Verify one wallet by read-only balance check

The member access page checks GCA balance with a read-only ERC-20 balanceOf call on Base Mainnet. It does not require a transaction or signature.

Enter the correct review lane

10,000 GCA can enter holder bonus review. 1,000,000 GCA held for 30 consecutive days can enter GCA Member and member benefit review.

Wait for support review

Support checks account uniqueness, wallet evidence, holding period, and member benefit evidence before any manual reserve-wallet transfer is processed.
